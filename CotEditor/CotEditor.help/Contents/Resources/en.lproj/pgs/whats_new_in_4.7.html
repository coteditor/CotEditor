What’s new in CotEditor 4.7?

Perform actions quickly from your keyboard
With the new Quick Action feature, you can perform menu commands just by typing few letters on your keyboard into the command bar. See “Perform action by typing” for details.
Import text by Continuity Camera
Using the camera on your iPhone or iPad, you can import text in the real-word to CotEditor documents on Mac. See “Insert text from camera” for details.
