Print preferences

You can change the default print options here.
Changes you make here will take effect when you next create/open a document.

Font
You can either use the document's font or specify another font for printing purpose.
Color
You can choose between Same as Document's Setting, Black and White, and your favorite themes.
Line numbers
You can choose between Don't Print, Same as Document's Setting, and Print.
Invisibles
You can choose between Don't Print, Same as Document's Setting, and Print.
Header and Footer
You can configure whether to print, what to print, and where to print.
