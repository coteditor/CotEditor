Manage warnings in CotEditor on Mac

You can turn off some warnings in the CotEditor app on your Mac in the settings.

To change the settings, go to General settings and click the Manage Warnings button under the dialog warnings section.

OptionDescription
Inconsistent line endings

Suppress the warning dialog that appears when opening a document with mixed line ending codes. It is not recommended to suppress this dialog unless you need to open documents that have inconsistent line endings frequently intentionally.
