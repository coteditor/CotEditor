If your keyboard shortcut doesn’t work as you expect in CotEditor on Mac

If a shortcut you set doesn’t work as expected, one of the following may be the cause:

  • The shortcut is already taken by another function.

  • Entering a special character may take precedence over performing a command when you press a letter key with modifier keys.
