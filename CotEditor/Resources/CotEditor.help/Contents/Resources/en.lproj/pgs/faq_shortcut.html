If your keyboard shortcut doesn’t work as you expect in CotEditor on Mac

If a shortcut you set doesn’t work as expected, one of the following may be the cause:

  • The shortcut is already assigned to another command.

  • For certain combinations of character keys and modifier keys, text input takes precedence over invoking the command.
