Print a document in CotEditor on Mac

You can change your print settings in the CotEditor section in the Print dialog. The settings can be saved as a preset in the Print dialog.

To print your document, choose File > Print.

  1. Go to the CotEditor app on your Mac.

  2. Open a document you want to print.

  3. To print only part of a document, select the text you want to print.

  4. Choose File > Print.

  5. Choose print options.

  6. Click Print.
