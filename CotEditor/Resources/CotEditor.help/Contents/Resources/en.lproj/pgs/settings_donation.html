Change Donation settings in CotEditor on Mac

In the CotEditor app on your Mac, use Donation settings to support the CotEditor project by offering coffee.

To change these settings, choose CotEditor > Settings, then click Donation.

OptionDescription
Continuous support

A recurring donation to the CotEditor project. The subscription renews automatically each year. To cancel it, click Manage Subscriptions and cancel it in the App Store. If you already donated before, you can also restore the subscription.
Badge type

Select the badge shown on the status bar while continuous support is active.
One-time donation

A one-time donation to the CotEditor project. You can make it more than once, but it doesn’t include extras such as the coffee badge for continuous support.

  • Donations are handled through in-app purchases on the App Store provided by Apple. This feature is available only in CotEditor downloaded from the App Store.

  • An internet connection is required to donate or restore purchase information.

Note: All donations made here go directly to the individual developer.
