Uninstall CotEditor on Mac

You can uninstall CotEditor the same way you would uninstall any standard macOS app.

  1. Quit the CotEditor app on your Mac.

  2. In the Finder, move CotEditor to the Trash.

Completely uninstall CotEditor

To uninstall CotEditor and remove all related files, delete the following files and folders:

  • /Applications/CotEditor

  • ~/Library/Containers/com.coteditor.CotEditor/

  • ~/Library/Application Scripts/com.coteditor.CotEditor/

Here, the tilde (~) represents your home folder and the slash (/) means the folder separator.
