Perform action by typing in CotEditor on Mac

With the Quick Action feature, you can perform menu commands just by typing few keys on your keyboard.

To perform a quick action:

  1. Go to the CotEditor app on your Mac.

  2. Launch the command bar either by choosing View > Show Quick Actions or by pressing Command-K.

  3. Type characters into the command bar. Then, the matched actions are shown incrementally below the field.

    It matches the menu commands having all the typed characters in the order in the intermediate submenus and its title.

  4. Select the action to perform in the list by pressing the ↓ or ↑ key.

  5. Press the Return key to perform the selected action.

    To close the command bar without taking any action, do one of the following:

    • Press Command-K (again).

    • Press Esc.

    • Press Command-Period (.).

    • Press Return while no action candidates are shown.

    • Click somewhere outside of the command bar.
